Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from Zoom's built-in noise suppression?
Zoom and other platforms use basic noise suppression that either blocks everything (making voices robotic) or lets disruptive sounds through. Our AI specifically identifies and eliminates problem sounds like dogs, kids, and typing while preserving natural voice quality and important audio.
Do I need special hardware or software to use MeetingMute AI?
No! Our browser extension works on any computer with Chrome, Firefox, or Safari. Unlike RTX Voice which requires expensive NVIDIA graphics cards, MeetingMute AI runs efficiently on standard hardware using cloud-based AI processing.
Will this slow down my video calls or cause delays?
Not at all. Our AI processes audio in real-time with less than 10ms latency - imperceptible to you and your call participants. The processing is highly optimized and won't impact your computer's performance.
What video platforms does MeetingMute AI work with?
MeetingMute AI works with all browser-based video platforms including Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, WebEx, GoToMeeting, and dozens of others. If it runs in your browser, we support it.
Can I try it before paying?
Absolutely! We offer a generous free tier with 2 hours per day, plus a 14-day free trial of Pro features. No credit card required to start. We're confident you'll love the results.
